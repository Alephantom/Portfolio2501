## 🥢 Quick & Healthy Vegetable Stir-Fry with Chicken  

A **delicious, protein-packed stir-fry** that’s perfect for busy weeknights. This recipe is **easy to make, full of nutrients, and customizable** to fit whatever veggies you have in your fridge!

### 🕒 **Servings & Time**
- **Serves:** 2-3
- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 15-20 minutes

## 🛒 Ingredients  

### **For the Stir-Fry:**
- 250g boneless, skinless **chicken breast** or thighs (sliced thin)
- 1 tbsp **cornstarch**  
- 2 tbsp **vegetable oil** (divided)  
- 1 **red bell pepper** (sliced)  
- 1 **medium carrot** (julienned or sliced)  
- 100g **broccoli florets**  
- 100g **snap peas**  
- 100g **mushrooms** (sliced)  

### **For the Sauce:**
- 3 tbsp **soy sauce**  
- 1 tbsp **hoisin sauce** *(optional, for sweetness)*  
- 1 tsp **sesame oil**  
- 1 tsp **grated ginger**  
- 2 **garlic cloves**, minced  
- 1 tsp **cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water** *(for thickening)*  

### **Optional Garnish:**  
- **Sesame seeds**  
- **Chopped spring onions**  

---

## 🍳 Instructions  

### **1️⃣ Prep the Chicken**
- Slice the chicken **against the grain** into thin strips.  
- Toss with **1 tbsp cornstarch** for a tender, velvety texture.  

### **2️⃣ Cook the Chicken**  
- Heat **1 tbsp vegetable oil** in a wok or large pan over **medium-high heat**.  
- Cook the chicken for **5-6 minutes**, flipping occasionally until golden and fully cooked.  
- Remove and set aside.  

### **3️⃣ Stir-Fry the Vegetables**  
- Add the remaining **1 tbsp oil** to the pan.  
- Stir-fry **bell pepper, carrot, and broccoli** for **3-4 minutes** until they start to soften.  
- Add **snap peas & mushrooms**, cooking for another **2 minutes**.  

### **4️⃣ Make the Sauce**  
- In a small bowl, mix **soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and cornstarch slurry**.  
- Pour over the vegetables and stir to coat evenly.  
- Cook for **1-2 minutes** until the sauce thickens.  

### **5️⃣ Combine & Serve**  
- Add the cooked **chicken back into the pan** and toss everything together.  
- Serve **hot** over **steamed rice, noodles, or quinoa**.  
- Garnish with **sesame seeds & spring onions** (optional).  

---

## 🔥 **Pro Tips: Perfect Stir-Fry Chicken**  

### **✅ Cut Evenly**
- Slice **thin & against the grain** for tender, easy-to-cook chicken.  

### **✅ Marinate for More Flavor** *(Optional but recommended!)*  
Toss the sliced chicken with:  
- **1 tbsp soy sauce**  
- **1 tsp cornstarch** *(for texture)*  
- **1 tsp sesame oil** *(optional)*  
- **1 tsp rice vinegar or lemon juice** *(for tenderness)*  
Let it marinate for **15-30 minutes** before cooking.  

### **✅ Pre-Cook the Chicken**
- Heat your wok **hot** and sear in **small batches** (avoid overcrowding).  
- Sear for **2-3 minutes per side**, then remove and set aside.  

### **✅ Avoid Soggy Chicken**
- **Pat dry** before cooking (if not marinating) to ensure **good browning**.

## 🍽️ **Make It Your Own!**  

- 🌶️ **Spice it up**: Add chili flakes or sriracha for extra heat!  
- 🥦 **Go fully vegetarian**: Swap chicken for tofu or double up on veggies.  
- 🍋 **Fresh twist**: Squeeze some lemon or lime juice before serving.
